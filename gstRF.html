WHAT IS

GST Return Filing ?

Every person who is registered under GST is required to file GSTR-1 (Return of outward supply) till the 10th day of the subsequent month or quarter-end, GSTR-3B (monthly summary return) till the 20th of the subsequent month.

Traders and Manufacturers registered under the GST composition scheme are required to file GSTR-4 every quarter, till the 18th of the subsequent month next to the quarter. In the end, annual GST return is to be filed by all GST registered entities before the 31st of December of the subsequent financial year.

Further, all the GST returns that may be GSTR-1, GSTR-2A, GSTR-3B, GSTR-4, GSTR-8 or annual return are internally linked to each other due to which filing and reconciling of the data in an accurate method is very important.

Team Legalworkmate offers an end to end GST services from registration to return filing along with GST reconciliation. Legalworkmate helps you to file your monthly, and annual GST returns. Filing proper GST returns under the GST regime is crucial as non-compliance and delay can result in penalties and affect your compliance rating and refunds.

Frequently Asked Questions

General

WHO ARE REQUIRED TO FILE GST RETURN?

GST returns are to be filed by every person who is registered under the GST irrespective of any supply made or not. GST return is also required to be filled even if there is no business commenced.

WHAT IS GSTR 3B AND WHEN IT HAS TO BE FILLED?

GSTR 3B is a summary return and has to be filled on a monthly basis irrespective of the turnover. GSTR 3B contains the data of the total supply made during a month and the total GST credit available.

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN GSTR 1 AND GSTR-3B?

GSTR 3B is a summary return and contains only the amount in total. Whereas GSTR 1 is a detailed return and invoice wise details of the inward and outward supply.

WHO ARE REQUIRED TO FILE MONTHLY GSTR-1 GST RETURN?

It is mandatory to file monthly GSTR-1 for registered people having a monthly turnover of more than INR 1.5 Crores.

IS COPY OF INVOICE UPLOADED WITH THE GST RETURN?

No, Copy of return is not required to be uploaded with the GST return only specific prescribed fields are required to be updated.

IS COPY OF INVOICE UPLOADED WITH GST RETURN?

No, Copy of return is not required to be uploaded with the GST return only specific prescribed fields are required to be updated.

WHAT IS RECONCILATION OF GST CREDIT?

Input credit filled by the supplier is reflected in GSTR 2A automatically with details of all the input supply. The Credit reflected in GSTR 2A has to be reconciled with that shown in books of accounts, GSTR 3B and GSTR 1 quarterly.

WHICH GST RETURN IS TO BE FILED BY COMPOSITE TAXABLE PERSON?

Composite Taxable person has to file a Quarterly GSTR 4 return till 18th of the month subsequent to that quarter. There is no requirement to file GSTR 3 or GSTR 3B every month.

DO I HAVE TO PROVIDE SALES DATA FOR B2X=C SALES IN DETAIL?

Generally the only total amount of sales is required in the case of B2C sales. But details are required in case of inter-state supplies having invoice value of more than Rs. 2.50 lakhs.

IS THERE ANY LATE FILING FEES IN CASE OF DELAY IN SUBMISSION OF NIL RETURN?

Yes, there is a late filing fee of INR 20/- per day in case of composition filing of GST return in case of NIL GST return filing.

WHAT IS AN ANNUAL GST RETURN?

Section 44 of CGST Act requires every person registered under the GST to file annual GST return till 31st December of the subsequent financial year. Various annual returns are as under

  • GSTR 9Filled by a person who is required to file GSTR 1 and whose turnover is less than INR 2 Crores.

  • GSTR 9AFilled by person who has filled GST return under the composite scheme.

  • GSTR 9CThis return is to be filled by the person whose turnover exceeds INR 2 Crores in a financial year. This return is also called a GST Audit return.

WHAT IS THE AMOUNT OF GST PAYABLE IN CASE OF EXPORT OF GOODS OR SERVICE?

In the case of Export of Goods or Service, the person is liable to pay Zero amount of GST if the same is done through Letter Of Undertaking.

WHO CAN CLAIM A REFUND OF GST AMOUNT PAID?

Registered people whose monthly turnover is more than INR 1.5 Crores is liable to file monthly GSTR-1 return.
