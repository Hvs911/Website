Starting at INR 8,500/-

It is mandatory for a large organisation having employees more than 10 or 20 to obtain registration under PF and ESIC Act. The act applies to almost all types of business and is no more limited to factories.

Trademark Registration

Protecting the name of a brand that you have created is the most important thing for business. A trademark helps a business not only to protect infringement of its name but, also to create goodwill in the market.

Starting at INR 750/- P.M.

It is mandatory for all the types of business entity except individual not liable to audit to deduct TDS from payment of Interest, Rent, contract fees, professional fees, foreign remittance etc.

Income Tax Filing.

GST Compliance.

Starting at INR 500/- P.M.

If you have obtained GST registration, then it is mandatory for you to file your GST return on or before due date whether any business has taken place or not. If not filled then penalty will be levied.

New company mandatory compliance

At INR 2,000/-.

Once a company is registered, it is mandatorily required to hold its first board meeting, appoint an Auditor, and File commencement certificate to the ROC.

Company ROC Compliance.

Starting at INR 4000/- Yearly

Every company is required to file 4 to 5 ROC forms, hold board meetings, prepare board report, and hold AGM in a year. Non-compliance of this will result in penalty and strike-off of the name.

View More

LLP ROC Compliance.

Starting at INR 4,000/- Yearly

Every LLP is required to file 2 to 4 ROC forms, File IT return and do KYC of its partners. Non-compliance of this will result in penalty and strike-off of the name.
