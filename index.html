Private Limited Company

At INR 4,900/-

Private Limited Company is the default choice of all the startups as they can raise equity funds from investors. Besides, all the directors and shareholders have limited liability.

Limited Liability Partnership

At INR 4,500/-

LLP is a most suitable form of business for professionals and business not intending to go for equity funding. It offers an advantage of Limited Liability of partners.

One Person Company

At INR 4,100/-

Only structure for a proprietor to take advantage of limited liability and get recognition of a company. With this a promoter can also have full authority over entire business.

Partnership Firm

At INR 2,360/-

A business structure that can be easily started by two people merely by executing a deed. Most suitable for a business who wants to operate on a small scale or want to get idea of new market
